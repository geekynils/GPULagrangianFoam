Detailed Description

Implements particle tracking as described in the paper by Graham B. Macpherson, Niklas Nordin and Henry G. Weller. "Particle tracking in unstructured, arbitrary polyhedral meshes for use in CFD and molecular dynamics." - 2008.

The calculation of lambda_c and lambda_a is executed on the GPU.
